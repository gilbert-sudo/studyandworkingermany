## Supported Ausbildung Categories & Sectors
The application must strictly support, categorize, and validate data based on the following 10 professional sectors and their respective job titles:

1. **Handwerk (Crafts & Trades)**
   - Tischler:in (Carpenter)
   - Maurer:in (Mason)
   - Zimmerer:in (Carpenter/Joiner)
   - Fliesenleger:in (Tiler)
   - Maler:in (Painter)
   - Bäcker:in (Baker)
   - Steinmetz:in (Stonemason)
   - Anlagenmechaniker:in für Sanitär-, Heizungs- und Klimatechnik (SHK) (HVAC Technician)

2. **Metall & Werkzeugmaschinenbau (Metalworking & Machine Construction)**
   - Konstruktionsmechaniker/in (Construction Mechanic)
   - Metallbauer/in (Metalworker)
   - Maschinen- und Anlagenführer:in (Machine and Plant Operator)

3. **Elektrotechnik (Electrical Engineering)**
   - Elektroniker (Electrician)
   - Industrieelektriker/in (Industrial Electrician)
   - Mechatroniker/in (Mechatronics Engineer)
   - Elektroniker:in für Energie- und Gebäudetechnik (Electronics Technician for Energy and Building Technology)

4. **Baugewerbe (Civil Engineering & Construction)**
   - Straßenbauer (Road Builder)
   - Kanalbauer (Pipe Layer / Sewer Builder)
   - Beton- und Stahlbetonbauer/in (Concrete and Reinforced Concrete Worker)
   - Dachdecker:in (Roofer)

5. **Einzelhandel (Retail)**
   - Retail professions and specialists

6. **Hotellerie / Gastronomie (Hospitality & Catering)**
   - Hotel and culinary professions

7. **Pflegeberufe / Medizinischer Bereich (Healthcare & Nursing)**
   - Pflegeberufe (Nursing professions)
   - Berufe im medizinischen Bereich (Medical sector professions)
   - Physiotherapeuten (Physiotherapists)

8. **Berufskraftfahrer/in (Professional Driver)**
   - Commercial and heavy vehicle drivers

9. **Kaufmännische Berufe (Commercial & Business Professions)**
   - Business administration, clerical, and office management roles

10. **Lagerlogistik (Warehouse Logistics)**
    - Fachkraft für Lagerlogistik (Logistics Specialist)
    - Fachlagerist (Warehouse Operator)

## Language Requirements
The agency does **not** teach any languages. Clients must already possess a valid German language certificate. The accepted and verified certificates are mainly from:
- **Goethe-Institut**
- **Telc**
